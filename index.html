What is DROPEARNER


Welcome to our Innovation parcel delivery platform! Our website provides a unique opportunity for users to earn money by delivering parcels. Users can post their delivery requests, specifying the route and details. Couriers can then accept these requests and negotiate the delivery price directly with the sender. Our platform facilitates secure and efficient transactions, connecting senders and couriers seamlessly. Join us in revolutionizing the way parcels are delivered while earning extra income. Embrace a reliable, community-driven solution that brings convenience and trust to the world of parcel delivery.
